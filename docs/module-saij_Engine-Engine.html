saij/Engine~Engine 


import Engine from 'saij/Engine';

The saij Engine is the coordinator of all the organs. Using this architecture the organs themselves will have their own brains, and they are interconnected by the engine.

The engine can be used with a clock to signify a rhythm in the data flow, or it will default to asynchronous delivery of data.
